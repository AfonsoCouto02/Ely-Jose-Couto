SEEKING REPRESENTATION

About the Novel

Sylvia Lake has always known something was different about her. What she calls "the Nameless" lives beneath her skin, a presence both terrifying and strangely comforting. But when the world around her begins to shift in ways that mirror her inner turmoil, Sylvia must confront the truth about what she carries within.

In this gripping science fiction thriller, the boundaries between self and other, reality and perception, begin to blur. As Sylvia delves deeper into the mystery of the Nameless, she discovers that her burden may be the key to understanding forces far greater than herself.
